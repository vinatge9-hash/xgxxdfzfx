Our Story

We began as two friends with a shared reverence for a good cup. We visit farms, cup every lot, and roast in small batches so you experience a cup with origin and season intact. Our mission is to center transparency and craft while supporting regenerative practices.

Sourcing with Care

We form direct relationships with farmers — pre-financed crop planning, fair pricing, and shared quality goals. Each bag includes batch notes and roast date so you know when it was roasted.

  • • Direct trade & living wages
  • • Regenerative farming practices
  • • Small-batch roasts with roast-date transparency
